Part 1 图表写作
The graph below shows the average monthly change in prices of copper, nickel and zinc in 2014. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
学生作答内容
The graph illustrates the average monthly change in the prices of copper, nickel and zinc in 2014. Overall, the prices of nickel showed the greatest fluctuation during the given period, while the changes for copper and zinc were relatively more stable. In January, the price change for nickel was highest at 6%, but it dropped significantly to -3% in June. By the end of the year, all three metals experienced a slight increase in their price changes.
Part 2 议论文写作
Some people say an ageing population creates problems for governments. Others think there are benefits if society has more elderly people. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
学生作答内容
It is widely debated whether an ageing population is a burden or a blessing for a society. While some argue that a growing number of elderly citizens puts pressure on government resources, others believe that they can offer valuable contributions. In my opinion, although there are economic challenges, the benefits of an older population outweigh the drawbacks.

On the one hand, an ageing population can strain public services. Governments have to allocate more funds to healthcare and pensions, which might lead to higher taxes for the working population. On the other hand, older people possess a wealth of experience and knowledge. They can contribute to society by volunteering, taking care of grandchildren, and providing guidance to younger generations. Therefore, their role in maintaining social stability should not be underestimated.

Part 3: Discussion
Do you think tourism has a positive or negative impact on local cultures? Why do some people prefer traveling alone rather than in a group?
学生作答内容
Well, I believe tourism has both positive and negative impacts. On the positive side, it brings economic growth and helps preserve historical sites. However, it can also lead to commercialization. As for traveling alone, I think people prefer it because it offers more freedom and flexibility to change plans without compromising with others.
